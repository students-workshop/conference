UIPE Students' Workshop

October 8th, 2020

COVID-19 has radically changed the world as we understand it, requiring us to rapidly adapt to the new world we live in. Engineering among other fields is one of the fronts being leveraged to combat this threat. In this spirit, this students’ workshop is themed around COVID-19 and how, we as engineering students, can play a role in addressing the challenges created by COVID-19. We invite students from around the country to attend this workshop and share ideas on how they can use the skills they have attained thus far to make the best of this time and looking forward post-COVID-19.

Essay Competition

We are pleased to announce an essay competition, this competition is open to all interested participants however it is not a requirement to attend the workshop.

Interested participants will write an essay of 200 - 250 expressing ideas to answer the question; In what ways can engineering be leveraged to combat COVID-19?

The top three essays will present their essays and consequently be awarded a token of appreciation during the workshop as below;
● First Place UGX 200, 000
● Second Place UGX 150, 000
● Third Place UGX 100,000
